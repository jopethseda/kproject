PLOT OF THE COMIC

"The Virago" is a webtoon version of the comic strip the creators have already made as part of their project for English. The comic strip they have made is a modern adaptation of the original Canterbury tale, The Merchant's Tale, so they have decided to impose the concept into the website. Therefore, the theme of the website is minimalistic, modern, and simple but not simpler.

PLOT OF THE ORIGINAL TALE

The Merchant's Tale tells the great difference between the patient Griselde (The Clerk's Tale) and the horrible virago he married for the past two months. The merchant was asked to tell a story of the evils of marriage, and so he followed.

His story revolves around an old blind knight named January who married a beautiful young woman called May despite his friends disapproval. He tried to satisfy his woman's sexual needs but failed to do so. His wife, sexually unsatisfied, then conspires to cheat on him with Damien, his landlord.

When blind January decided to take his wife into his garden to have sexual intercourse with her, but she told him she wanted to eat a pear so he helped her up in the tree, where she and Damien had a sexual intercourse instead. But Pluto, the king of the fairies restores January's sight. But May, caught red-handed, convinces her husband that his sight is deceiving him. The Host prays to God to prevent him from marrying a shrew, like how the Merchant described the wife in his tale.
