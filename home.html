ABOUT THE WEBTOON

"The Virago" is a webtoon version of the comic strip the creators have already made as part of their project for English. The comic strip they have made is a modern adaptation of the original Canterbury tale, The Merchant's Tale, so they have decided to impose the concept into the website. Therefore, the theme of the website is minimalistic, modern, and simple but not simpler.
